Mountain Biking at Tsali

Tsali is a system of mountain bike trails in Western North Carolina on the edge of the Smokey Mountains. I recetly took a trip there with a couple of friends. Following are pictures from the trip:

Catching a little air Cruising Fontana Lake
In these photos, he's catching a little air off of a jump located on the Right Loop of the Tsali trail, as well as cruising by Fontana Lake on the Left Loop. The jump picture shows the last in a series that has provided a change of pace from the fast descents and smooth climbs. The cruise picture shows how tha water in the lake has lowered due to dam maintenence.
