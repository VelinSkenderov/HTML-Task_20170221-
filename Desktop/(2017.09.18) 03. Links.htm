Arc-Shot

A shot in which the subject is photographed by an ancircling or moving camera

Interlude

A brief, intervening film scene or sequence, not specifically tied to the plot, that appears within a film

Prologue

A speech, preface, introduction, or brief scene preceeding the main action or plot of a film; contrast to epilogue
