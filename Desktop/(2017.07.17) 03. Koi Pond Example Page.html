A Backyard Koi Pond

This is my koi pond Web page. Koi are ornamental Japanese carp that are specially bread in a variety of different vibrant colors. The koi in my pond come from from all over the world: some are from Japan, some from Israel, a few from Australia, and most of the little ones were born right here in my backyard.
