Rules:

Q: Can I sell employees to other players, transfer them between businesses, or turn them back in the Employee Pool for money?

A: No, you can't sell employees to other players or transfer them between different businesses. Employees can be turned back into the Employee Pool (laid off) but you don't receive any money.

Q: What happens if I land on the Corporate Headhunter space and I can't use all of the employees I receive?

A: The employees must be returned to the Employee Pool.
