Touch screen monitors


Touch screen is probably the most important component in our kiosks

All the samplers of KIOSKY kiosks are finished with monitors walling up of a highest grade and with a quality already proven of Elo, Microtouch 3M and Nexio. 

Walling up monitors of a highest grade allows the terminals' working process to be of long standing and without complications, so as a perfect picture and a touchscreen extremely comfortable for using.

Touchscreen based on the SAW technology (Surface Acoustic Wave) has been used most popularly at information terminals. However, almost the whole bunch of touchscreen technologies is forthcoming such as capacitive, resistive, infrared, etc.


Interface of monitors is USB and operating system has automatically recognized it.
