WHO WE ARE

Business Management Systems is an information technology and services company, providing full-scope solutions for business process automation.

Our company is certified in ISO 9001:2015 (view certificate) and ISO/IEC 27001:2014 (view certificate) standards.

We at Business Management Systems aim to continuously expand, becoming an important provider of Information Technology and Communications solutions and services. We rely on the capabilities of our consultants and their over ten years’ experience in the wide range of business solutions we offer to our clients. As a part of Great Goodwin Gate Holding GmbH, a multinational company with branches in Austria, Bulgaria and Romania, we provide the full scope of Information Technology and Communications services, with customizable projects and solutions dedicated to large and medium-sized enterprise customers throughout Europe.
