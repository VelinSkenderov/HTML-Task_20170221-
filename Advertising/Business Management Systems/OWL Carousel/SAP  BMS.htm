Integrated enterprise resource planning systems

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) - integrated information system for planning and management of all of the enterprise’s resources such as planning, budgeting and control in the areas of production, delivery, sales, storage, resources, projects and accounting.

The ERP system enforces operative control in all fields of company activity thus enabling solid grounds for decision making on all managerial levels both short term and long term.

The ERP system allows management to perform a comprehensive analysis of the company’s activity in different aspects by tracking results and controlling factors affecting business performance indicators.

SAP ERP

Integrated system for business management

  • Encompasses all business processes in the enterprise and automates the activities of management of finance and accounting, trading, manufacturing, equipment maintenance, human resource and customer and partner relations with the organization
  • Provides credible information about the company’s financial status for the purpose of strategic planning and financial forecasting
  • Provides means for system administration and for resolution of tasks as user management, centralized management of data
  • Provides opportunity for integration with other business systems

Functionality

  • Controlling (CO)

  • Plant maintenance (PM)
  • Human resource (HR)
  • Workflow (WF)
  • Document management system (DMS)
